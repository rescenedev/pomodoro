Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

Pomodoro is a native macOS menu-bar timer. Your privacy matters, and the app is built to need none of your personal data.

What we collect

Nothing. Pomodoro does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising.

Data stored on your device

Your preferences — timer durations, cycle length, auto-start, and sound choice — are saved locally on your Mac using the standard macOS preferences system (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your computer and is removed if you delete the app and its preferences.

Network access

The app does not connect to the internet and makes no network requests.

Notifications

With your permission, Pomodoro posts local notifications when a session ends. These are handled entirely by macOS on your device and are not sent anywhere.

Third parties

Pomodoro includes no third-party SDKs, trackers, or analytics services.

Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
